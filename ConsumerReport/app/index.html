YOUR RESULTS

You have 2 options for coverage

Option 1

Marketplace with Tax Credit

In the new Marketplace you can compare and buy private health insurance.

The Marketplace is the only place you can get federal subsidies to help pay for health insurance.

Your Next Steps

  • 1.

    Check with your Marketplace to see if anything else is required.

  • 2.

    Fill out an application at the New York State Health Benefit Exchange (healthbenefitexchange.ny.gov).

  • 3.

    Consider taking advantage of the tax credit. You can apply the credit in two ways: either as a refund on your 2014 tax credit or as an advance payment tax credit now.

TAKE NOTE

If you use the advance payment tax credit to help lower the cost of coverage, be sure to let the Marketplace know if:

  • Your INCOME goes up or down
  • ou get married or have a child
  • You get a job that offers affordable Health Insurance
